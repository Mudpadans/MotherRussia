The Alphabet

  • A - "ah" - "Ананас" - "Ananas" - Pineapple
  • Б - "be" - "Бабочка" - "Babochka" - Butterfly
  • В - "ve" - "Вода" - "Voda" - Water
  • Г - "ge" - "Город" - "Gorod" - City
  • Д - "de" - "Дом" - "Dom" - House
  • Е - "ye" - "Ель" - "Yel'" - Tree
  • Ё - "yo" - "Ёлка" - "Yolka" - pine Tree
  • Ж - "zhe" - "Жук" - "Zhuk" - Beetle
  • З - "ze" - "Заяц" - "Zayats" - Hare
  • И - "ee" - "Иголка" - "Igolka" - Needle
  • Й - "eektratkaya" - "Йогурт" - "Yogurt" - Yogurt
  • К - "ke" - "Кот" - "Kot" - Cat
  • Л - "le" - "Луна" - "Luna" - Moon
  • М - "me" - "Мир" - "Mir" - peace
  • Н - "ne" - "Нос" - "Nos" - Nose
  • О - "o" - "Окно" - "Okno" - Window
  • П - "pe" - "Парк" - "Park" - Park
  • Р - "rre (rolled)" - "Речка" - "Rechka" - River
  • С - "se" - "Солнце" - "Solntse" - Sun
  • Т - "te" - "Телефон" - "Telefon" - Telephone
  • У - "oo" - "Ухо" - "Ukho" - Ear
  • Ф - "fe" - "Флаг" - "Flag" - Flag
  • Х - "he" - "Хлеб" - "Hleb" - Bread
  • Ц - "tse" - "Цветок" - "Tsvetok" - Flower
  • Ч - "che" - "Часы" - "Chasiy" - Clock
  • Ш - "she" - "Шутка" - "Shootka" - joke
  • Щ - "shye (harder sh)" - "Щенок" - "Sheenok" - puppy
  • Ъ - "tvyordi znak (hardens the ending consonant)" - "Объект" - "Ob'yekt" - Object
  • Ы - "(as in ill)" - "Рыба" - "Ryba" - Fish
  • Ь - miyahki znak "softens the ending consonant" - "Лень" - "Len'" - Laziness
  • Э - "eh" - "Экран" - "Ekran" - Screen
  • Ю - "u" - "Юбка" - "Yoobka" - skirt
  • Я - "ya" - "Яблоко" - "Yabloko" - Apple
